Why Should I Be A Christian?

There are so many benefits to being a Christian. First off, you get to live the life that you were created to live: one of freedom and of praising God. If you accept Jesus Christ as your Lord and savior, then you will be able to get into heaven when you die because all of your sins will be forgiven! Also, if you let Jesus guide your life, then you will bring joy and happiness to yourself and to those around you. Find more information on the benefits of Christianity here.

Can Jesus Really Forgive All My Sins?

Yes, he can! As long as you have accepted Jesus into your heart, then all the wordly sins you commit will be forgiven. On the flip side, however, the one unforgivable sin is not believing that God sent down Jesus to save us all, or "blasphemy against the Spirit."
